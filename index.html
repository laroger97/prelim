WELCOME TO CITY CAMP PROPER!!!

One of the best performing Barangay in Baguio City

City Camp Proper Barangay is one of the best performing barangay in Baguio City since 2009. One of the barangays in baguio with the least crime rate, surely one of the safest place to live in! With a population of 185,548 we asure that no one is left out. We are not only a barangay, but we are family.

A smoke free barangay

Due to health reasons and because we care for our family. We are strict on implementing the "smoke-free baguio". We care. If you want to stop smoking, just come to our barangay hall and let's talk about your journey to stoping. Because we are not just a barangay, but we are family. And we want the best for you!

A drug free community

We have the biggest issues on drugs here in Baguio and in our neighboring municipalities. City Camp Proper make sure that every person in the community is taken a good care of. We prioritize the well-beingness of one another. We discourage the use of illegal drugs by spreading love to each other. Because remember, we are not just a barangay, WE ARE family

A child friendly community

We make sure that no one is left behind. Specially the youth. Because the children in our community will make our baranggay better in the long run. We encourage parents in our community to have better relationship with their children and every person in our community to befriend the young children.
